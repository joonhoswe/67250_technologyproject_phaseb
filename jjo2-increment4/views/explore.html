Mission Statement

MonoMuse is a museum dedicated to serving the community with carefully curated pieces and artworks from the past. Our mission is to serve the broader Pittsburgh community, and to teach the community about the intersection of technology and art.

Milestones

  • 2021 - MonoMuse gets equipped with Virtual Reality technology for more immersive experiences
  • 2019 - MonoMuse opens its doors to the Pittsburgh community.
